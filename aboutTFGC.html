Who Is the Two Face Gemini Collective

The two face Gemini Collective (TFGC) is a group of hip hop artists from the Greator Toronto and Hamilton Area's, who have collective joined together to not only over come the challenges of a competitive music scene, but also create a new welcoming community and platform where artists can meet, work together and grow.

At TFGC, we believe this is the ultimate way to acheive a common goal, shared passions, collectively!
